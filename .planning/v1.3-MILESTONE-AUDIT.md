## Integration Check

### FINDING-01: check_once excluded from job_runs (LOG-04 partial)

- **Location:** `main.py:376,392`
- **Detail:** check_once is added to scheduler directly, not via _run_with_lane_lock. All other scheduled jobs have job_runs tracking. This was a documented Phase 5 design decision.
- **Impact:** job_runs table is silent on the most frequent job (15-minute price monitoring cycle)
- **Recommendation:** Wrap check_once in a thin async wrapper with _try_db_job_start/_try_db_job_finish if operational observability is desired.
